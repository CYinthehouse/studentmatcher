📢 Instructions for Students

⏰ IMPORTANT: Set Your Alarm!

Please set an alarm for 10 minutes before your study session begins.

Once your alarm is set and you've opened a chat with your partner, proceed to the activities below.

1 Connect with Your Partner (2 minutes)

Add each other as friends and open a message chat with your study partner.

✔️ After adding each other as friends, type "1" in the chat to confirm this step is complete.

2 Ice Breaker Questions (6 minutes)

Take turns asking each other these questions:

  1. What made you want to learn Korean?
  2. The most difficult time you experienced in your life and how you overcame it.

💡 Listen carefully and be respectful of each other's stories.

3 Share Back (2 minutes)

After speaking with your partner, come back and share what you learned about each other.

🔒 Privacy Reminder: Share only general insights without going into personal details to respect your partner's privacy.
